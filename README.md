These files are the same as the ones in the MP_Substitutions__Main_Results repository except that here EPCI sequences had to meet stricter requirements in order to qualify. Specifically, in the main results, every sequences had to meet one of two requirements:

#1. Have a minimum of eight private amino acid amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges (where each private deletion range, no matter how large or small, counts as a single mutation) and be among the last 5% of sequences collected in the Pango lineage to which it belongs.
#2. Have a minimum of 10 private amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges

For Sensitivity_Test_Results_v1, the requirements are:

#1. Have a minimum of 10 private amino acid amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges (where each private deletion range, no matter how large or small, counts as a single mutation) and be among the last 5% of sequences collected in the Pango lineage to which it belongs.
#2. Have a minimum of 15 private amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges

For Sensitivity_Test_Results_v2, the requirements are:

#1. Have a minimum of 15 private amino acid amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges (where each private deletion range, no matter how large or small, counts as a single mutation) and be among the last 5% of sequences collected in the Pango lineage to which it belongs.
#2. Have a minimum of 20 private amino acid substitutions/deletion ranges
